About

Hi — I'm Natasha, and yoga found its way to my heart in Portland, Oregon in 2021. After quickly realizing its healing potential, I committed to life-long study and practice of the ancient wisdom. Most inspired by my teachers at the Bhakti Yoga Movement Center, my practice is rooted in loving devotion. As student and teacher now, my intention is to share the gift of yoga to bring us together. If you want to join hands in community, I'd love to practice with you.
